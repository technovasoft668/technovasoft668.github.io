Our application architecture is deeply anchored on user privacy. We build our software frameworks to minimize data collection. Depending on the functionalities of a specific app, the following data scopes may apply:

At NOVATECH SOFT, we deploy industry-recognized digital security baselines and localized encryption protocols to defend active application data environments. While no method of transmission over the open internet or electronic storage infrastructure is 100% impenetrable, we actively maintain modern developer sandboxing routines to fully secure user profiles against external threat actors or illegal data modifications.
