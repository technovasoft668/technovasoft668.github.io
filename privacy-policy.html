Privacy Policy

Nova Live by NOVATECH SOFT

Last Updated: July 2026

Welcome to Nova Live, developed and published by NOVATECH SOFT. Your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This document clearly outlines how we collect, manage, moderate, and secure your information when you interact with our live streaming and socialization application on the Google Play Store.

1. Permissions & Device Data Access

To provide core functionalities like live audio/video matching and profile customization, Nova Live requires specific, explicit permissions from your device. We strictly adhere to the principle of data minimization:

  • Camera Access: Required strictly for live video broadcasting and capturing profile avatars. We do not secretly record, intercept, or store your live video streams on our servers.
  • Microphone Access: Required for live audio rooms and voice matching. Your real-time audio is transmitted securely and is never recorded or saved by us.
  • Photo & Gallery Access: Required solely to allow you to upload custom profile pictures or room logos. We only access the specific media files you manually select.

2. Third-Party Integrations & SDKs

To ensure a seamless, high-quality, and secure runtime experience, Nova Live interfaces with globally trusted third-party SDKs. These independent entities govern data under their highly regulated architectures:

  • Google Firebase: Used for secure authentication (Google Sign-In, Anonymous Login), database storage (Cloud Firestore), and crash analytics. Firebase securely stores your public profile data (Nickname, Age, Gender, Avatar URL, and virtual Coin Balance).
  • ZegoCloud (Live SDK): Used to facilitate our real-time audio and video streaming protocols. ZegoCloud strictly acts as a transmission bridge and does not record your personal communication data.
  • Google AdMob: Manages policy-compliant rewarded video advertisements. AdMob may collect anonymous device identifiers to serve contextual ads safely.

3. User-Generated Content (UGC) & Safety Moderation

Nova Live is a platform for making friends and socializing. To maintain a safe, welcoming, and Google Play-compliant environment, we strictly enforce our UGC and Community Guidelines:

  • Strict Moderation: Hate speech, bullying, explicit content, and harassment are strictly prohibited. We employ ongoing moderation to monitor public room activities.
  • Report & Block Systems: You have full authority to "Block" users you do not wish to interact with. You can also use our in-app "Report User" and "Report Room" features.
  • Action & Enforcement: Our moderation team reviews reports within 24 hours. Violators of our UGC policy will face immediate and permanent device-level bans.

4. Data Retention & Account Deletion

You maintain absolute ownership and control over your personal data. We only retain your data as long as your account remains active on our platform.

  • Data Retention: Your profile data and virtual wallet balances are kept securely on Firebase cloud servers to maintain your account continuity.
  • Instant Account Deletion: You can permanently delete your account and all associated data directly from the app by navigating to Profile > Delete Account (In-App).
  • Permanent Erasure: Upon requesting deletion, your profile identity, coin balance, and history are immediately and irreversibly purged from our active database.

5. Age Restrictions & Children's Privacy

Nova Live is strictly built and restricted for users who are 18 years of age or older. We actively align with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not consciously harvest or retain digital identity footprints from anyone under 18. If a minor inadvertently bypasses our age gate, please contact us immediately for emergency data deletion.

6. Dynamic Policy Revisions

NOVATECH SOFT holds the legitimate right to scale or revise this legal Privacy Policy dynamically to stay compliant with evolving Google Play Developer Policies. Any structural modifications go live the moment the refreshed code is officially committed to this live website domain.
